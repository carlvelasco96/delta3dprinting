New Partners

There are two methods to become a new partner.

Method One

If your previous or current month's cumulative order value is $1000 or more you are eligible to become a partner. You can check this via your Profile Dashboard.

If you are eligible and wants to become a partner, simply click the 'Request partnership' button. It will instantaneously verify your eligibility, and if successful, it will upgrade your account status to 'Partner'.

Request partnership

Method Two

For new and existing customers looking to manufacture frequently with us but are currently ineligible due to lack of historical orders. You can still apply for partnership by contacting us through "Apply for partnership", detailing what you plan to manufacture frequently with us.

Apply for partnership

This only applies if you have never been a partner before. For returning partners, please follow the instructions under "Returning Partners".

Returning Partners

If a customer loses their partner status and wants to regain it, they simply require a cumulative order value of $1000 or more from the previous or current month.

If you are eligible and wants to become a partner, simply click the 'Request partnership' button. It will instantaneously verify your eligibility, and if successful, it will upgrade your account status to 'Partner'.

Request partnership

Requirements

To retain the partner status, partners must maintain an average monthly cumulative order value of $1000 or more. This is calculated by adding all order values from when the partner status was obtain until the current date. The total order value is then divided by the number months, counted from the month at which the partner status was obtained. The resulting value must equal or exceed $1000.

Calculation occur every 1st day of the month. And if the value calculated is lower than $1000, the partner status will be revoked. Partner status can be regained by follow the instructions under "Returning Partners".
